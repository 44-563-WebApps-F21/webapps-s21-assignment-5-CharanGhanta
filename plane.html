A fixed-wing aircraft propelled forward by thrust from a jet engine, propeller, or rocket engine is known as an airplane or aeroplane (informally plane). There are many different sizes, forms, and wing arrangements for airplanes. Recreation, transportation of goods and people, military, and research are just a few of the many purposes for airplanes.
